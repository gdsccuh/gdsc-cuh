GDSC at Central University of Haryana

Central University of Haryana, is located in Jant-Pali villages of Mahendragarh district of Haryana, India. By fostering a value-based, transparent work ethic, nurturing strong character, and encouraging critical and creative thinking, the university serve as a beacon of change people's overall development and ability to support themselves.
Developer Student Club @ CUH aims to educate people with ANY level of programming experience who are tech savvy and enthusiastic about technologies by providing a peer-to-peer learning environment.
